About Us

OUR STORY

Inspired by the indifference in resources available
to students, a group of students created onclass in
order to provide equal resources
for the student population.


OUR VISION

We hope to create a self sustaining student society,
with an abundance of resources and equal oppurtunities
for anyone who is looking for study material.


OUR STORY

We are an organization with no source of income.
However, if you could donate to our cause, it would help
us a lot in improving our infrastructure as well as
providing you with the best possible material.
